# Privacy Policy

Palma Minimal Launcher does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- **App Data**: The app reads your installed apps list only to display them in the launcher.
- **Preferences**: Your favorite app selections and order are stored locally on your device and are not shared.
- **Permissions**: The app requires the `QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES` permission to list your installed applications.

This app is designed to be fully functional offline and respects your privacy by not including any tracking or analytics.
